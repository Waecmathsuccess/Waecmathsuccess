Quiz: Natural Numbers, Fractions, and Decimals

1. What is a natural number?

A. Any number that can be negative.
B. A positive whole number including zero.
C. A positive whole number excluding zero.

A. 3
B. 5/7
C. 0.9

3. How do you convert a decimal like 0.75 to a fraction?

A. 75/100 = 3/4
B. 75/10
C. 7.5/10

4. Which of the following is a correct representation of the decimal 0.5?

A. 1/2
B. 5/10
C. Both A and B

5. Which of the following is NOT a natural number?

A. 5
B. -3
C. 100
