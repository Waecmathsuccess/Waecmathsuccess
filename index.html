About Breast Cancer

Breast cancer affects millions worldwide, and awareness can lead to early detection, better treatment, and improved survival rates. Learn about the symptoms, risks, and steps you can take to protect yourself and loved ones.

Recognizing Symptoms

  • Lump in the breast
  • Changes in the shape or size of the breast
  • Skin dimpling or texture change
  • Nipple retraction or inversion
  • Discharge from the nipple

Common Myths and Facts

  • Myth: Only women get breast cancer.
    Fact: Men can also get breast cancer, although it is less common.
  • Myth: Finding a lump means you have cancer.
    Fact: Not all lumps are cancerous, but they should be checked by a doctor.
  • Myth: Breast cancer only affects older people.
    Fact: While risk increases with age, younger individuals can also be diagnosed.
